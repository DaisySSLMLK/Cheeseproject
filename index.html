Gouda cheese (Dutch: Goudse kaas, "cheese from Gouda") is a sweet, creamy, yellow cow's milk cheese originating from the Netherlands. It is one of the most popular cheeses worldwide.
The name is used today as a general term for numerous similar cheeses produced in the traditional Dutch manner.

Dutch cheese makers generally use six gradations, or categories, to classify the cheese:

  • Young
  • Young matured
  • Matured
  • Extra matured
  • Old
  • Very old
